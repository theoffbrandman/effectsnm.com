EFFECTS

Elementary Fourth-Fifth Education and Collaborative Teaching of Science

Working to provide science to underserved students across Albuquerque

Click here to learn more

Mission

To inspire a passion in science in underserved schools in the Albuquerque area through inquiry-based learning and an early introduction to the scientific method.
